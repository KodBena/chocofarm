## Best-fit algorithms for depth>1 / D-live (verbatim)

- **RTT-driven delay control (TCP-Vegas / BBR-lite) of the single-threaded M/G/1 coalescing queue — static C5 (GATED on the one-line rtt wiring at runner:784)** — The formal object — delay control of the §3.3 single-thread server, which the model certifies IS an M/G/1-type queue ('forwards never overlap — THIS IS THE COALESCING ENGINE') — is the model's CENTRAL mechanism, not an analogy. The work-in-system proxy rtt_us·inflight (Little's law) is non-degenerate EXACTLY because depth>1 is live (the caveat: §0 depth-1 made inflight∈{0,1} and the proxy trivial; the inquiry regime makes the queueing estimate meaningful). rtt is the congestion PRICE the §3.3 queue generates that the NUM/Vegas convergence proof requires. Correction the model dictates: a cold-compile-aware RTT_min percentile-floor (§3.2/R2 inject one-time S_cold spikes that are NOT congestion). Heuristic flag: the depth>1 queueing dynamics the model does not re-derive.
- **Whittle-index restless-bandit gate (one shared index, per-thread (j,W) arms) — NEW, surfaced independently by static AND online families; RUNNABLE on today's live features** — The ONLY formulation pricing the §3.3 one-shared-server coupling correctly (T arms, one activation-constrained server) — the textbook restless-bandit setting no v2 candidate named. The per-thread arm state is the (in-flight-depth j, backlog W) chain that is multi-state ONLY under depth>1 (at depth-1 each arm is trivial and the RMAB degenerates — the caveat is precisely what gives it the structure it needs). The shared index is learned from ONE pooled reward but ACTS per-thread (dissolving the pool-reward-vs-per-thread-action confound), needs NO server-side observation, and is provably near-optimal (Weber-Weiss). Subsumes the ad-hoc 'deny top-p by submit_pressure' as the case where the rank key becomes the Whittle index. Heuristic flag: indexability rests on §6 monotonicity, proven only for depth-1 — a falsifiable hypothesis to check, not a settled fact, in the depth>1 regime.
- **Per-thread bang-bang / (s,S) threshold hysteresis on the (j,W) chain — static C1; supervised C0 threshold as its degenerate stump** — §6's monotone negative-feedback plant LICENSES threshold-optimality formally (monotone MDP ⇒ switching-curve policy optimal: Stidham-Weber survey, Topkis supermodularity), upgrading v2's 'binary actuator ⇒ relay' from convenience to plant-structure justification. The deny is the per-thread BURST-DEPTH truncator in the live regime (NOT a no-op as v2's §0-spine Con claimed): denying below r_lo truncates the burst at low j, allowing above r_hi lets j climb toward D. The override 'inflight==0 ⇒ force allow' is the j=0 reflecting barrier (the forced flush), not an ad-hoc bolt. Runnable on per-thread-state-only features. Heuristic flag: the monotone/indexability witness is proven only for the depth-1 fixed point.
- **Behavioral cloning of the interventional label_counterfactual_gate_advantage (do(gate) on the §5 coalescing-regime chain) — supervised C4** — The model + the 2026-06-19 empirical convoy update make this the FORMALLY-CORRECT label, not an expensive bolt-on. §6 negative-feedback self-correction CONFOUNDS every observational label (the plant self-recovers to its fixed point ~96-98% of the time regardless of the gate), so observational targets fit the autonomous recovery, not the action's effect — a textbook confounded-credit setting (Pearl). The deterministic fixture (§2 conforming closed system, byte-identical) is the literal do-operator that breaks the confound. The label = the advantage A^π on a Markov chain over §5 coalescing regimes whose collapse-tail is near-absorbing. Caveat flag: collect labels in BOTH drain-all and chunked regimes (under chunk_floor one flip also alters outstanding-message ordering — DOF-R reactivated — a strictly larger action-effect surface than the §0 spine admits).
- **Rare-event basin / collapse-onset classifier composed with all-allow (calibrated, focal-loss / one-class) — supervised NEW C5** — Rests on the SOLID, un-moved part of the model crossed with the 2026-06-19 empirics: the dps loss is CONCENTRATED in the §5-R4-like metastable collapse basin (~2-4% of pipelined windows, a well-fingerprinted region: B:88→1, server rows/forward ~130→1-3, every forward into the warmed 64-bucket), NOT spread over the operating range. A convex C1 / balanced-label C2 PROVABLY under-serves a measure-small non-convex tail; this SEPARATES job A (pick the R1 operating point — §6 self-solves it) from job B (catch the rare collapse — the actual dps tail). Composed with all-allow off-basin so it is a strict superset of the byte-identical baseline (ADR-0009-honest), reuses the existing collection harness (relabel, don't recollect), degrades to identity when uncertain. Caveat flag: the collapse may itself be a depth>1 / DOF-R phenomenon the §0 spine cannot represent, so its target lives partly in the superseded regime.
- **Learning-to-rank / submodular top-k deny-SET (pairwise RankNet / listwise LambdaMART / Set-Transformer scoring) — supervised NEW C6** — The CLEANEST formal warrant in any family because its objective rests entirely on the UN-MOVED part of the model: §5-R1 cross-thread-ONLY coalescing makes throughput a JOINT function of which ≤T threads align into a shared forward (denying A changes the value of denying B), so the v2 row-wise per-thread label is mis-specified at the margin. The deny-SET under a coalescing budget is a budgeted subset-selection over interacting threads — the submodular-maximization shape with greedy (1−1/e) (Nemhauser-Wolsey-Fisher), permutation-invariant by the §2.2 DEALER symmetry lifted to set-invariance. Strictly generalizes the pointwise C1/C2 (which is the ranker with a per-thread-independent score). Caveat note: only the per-thread depth>1 reading is approximate; the joint-objective grounding is on the solid part.
- **Lyapunov drift-plus-penalty / backpressure (MaxWeight) admission on the live `ready` queue — online NEW** — Needs ONLY the live `ready` queue lengths (sidesteps every sentinel-0 channel — server_rows_per_forward and rtt_us), inherits throughput-optimality-WITHOUT-arrival-rate-knowledge (Tassiulas-Ephremides / Neely), matching the family's 'reward is not in the observation' constraint. Cast in the SAME mathematics as the plant's own §6 stability proof (the absorbing-ceiling argument IS a V=0 Lyapunov drift bound; V>0 trades the model's stability slack for throughput). Unifies with the dual C5 (S*) and the Whittle index (λ) under one stability↔throughput parameter V — three reductions of one constrained-MDP object. The `ready` backlog is the queue the drift function penalizes; it is non-trivial ONLY under depth>1 (at depth-1 drain-all empties the queue every issue, degenerate signal — the caveat's live regime is what gives it a queue to manage). Runnable frontier.
