## The rate frontier (the numbers)

| | rate | %VoI | note |
|---|---|---|---|
| static floor | 0.0855 | 0% | fixed route, no sensing |
| exact decomp | 0.094 | +14% | first above the floor |
| **AZ best** | **~0.097–0.10** | **+20–27%** | first past decomp |
| clairvoyant ceiling | 0.1454 | 100% | **loose** — pays nothing to sense |

`%VoI = (rate − 0.0855)/(0.1454 − 0.0855)`. The clairvoyant ceiling is loose because
the whole difficulty (VoI gated behind deep contingent sensing chains) is the cost it
skips. The dual bound charges for that; its full-instance number is still pending (see
"Open work" #5).

This session's matched A/B (residual ON vs OFF, m=24, JAX-trained): **both plateau at
~0.10**. Residual capacity does not move the rate — consistent with "calibration, not
capacity." Data preserved in `runs/matched_resoff` (OFF) and `runs/matched_reson` (ON).
